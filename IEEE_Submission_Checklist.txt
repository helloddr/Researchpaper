IEEE Submission Checklist

1. Paper content matches code outputs exactly.
2. Tables inserted and numbered in final order.
3. Figures inserted and numbered in final order.
4. References converted to exact IEEE format.
5. Title, abstract, and keywords finalized.
6. Author name, affiliation, and email confirmed.
7. GitHub repository link verified.
8. Final proofreading completed.
9. Similarity/plagiarism check completed.
10. Advisor review completed if available.
11. Final manuscript placed into IEEE template.
12. All claims are supported by results.
